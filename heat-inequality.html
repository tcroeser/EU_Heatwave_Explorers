In Lyon, poorer neighbourhoods are systematically hotter and have less tree cover. Each dot is a 200m grid cell. The correlation between income and surface temperature is strong and highly significant (Spearman ρ = −0.40, p < 0.001). Tree canopy is the strongest single predictor of temperature (ρ = −0.53).
Sources: INSEE Filosofi 200m (2019), Landsat 9 LST (7 Aug 2022), Meta/WRI canopy (2020). Croeser, Rahman & Ghosh (2026), Nature Communications.

Land surface temperature during heatwave events

21 European cities — Landsat 9, 30m resolution. Dark = cool, bright = hot.

Surface temperature from Landsat 9 Collection 2 Level 2 (ST_B10), converted to °C. Heatwave scenes selected per city — most from summer 2024; UK cities from May 2026. Note: Landsat has a ~7-day processing lag, so these are from a past heatwave event, not the current one. Canopy: Meta/WRI 1m (2020). Unpublished analysis by Dr Thami Croeser, June 2026.
